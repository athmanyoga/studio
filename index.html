Our Story

A space built
for real people

"Yoga is not about touching your toes — it's about what you learn on the way down."

Athman Yoga Studio was born from a simple belief: that the practice of yoga should feel like coming home. We opened our doors with three teachers, two candles, and a lot of heart.

Today, our community has grown into hundreds of students at every level — from first-timers to dedicated daily practitioners. We keep our classes small, our teachers world-class, and our space genuinely welcoming.
